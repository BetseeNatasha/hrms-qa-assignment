## 2. Who is the most vulnerable person in this system, and what does a system failure mean for them specifically — not in the abstract, but this month?

The most vulnerable person is the construction worker. A payroll failure can result in delayed or incorrect salary payments, making it difficult to pay rent, buy food, support family members, or meet other monthly expenses.

## 3. Trace what happens when a site manager enters the wrong overtime hours for a worker. Where does that error travel, who should catch it, and who actually catches it?

The incorrect overtime entry moves from the site manager's attendance submission into payroll calculations and eventually affects the worker's salary. The first person to catch this should be the Payroll Operator who needs to verify the overtime calculations.
